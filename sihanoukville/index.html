Getting to Onederz Sihanoukville: A Guide for Our Guests

Onederz Sihanoukville is ideally located with easy access to the pier, offering convenience to boat departures and nearby attractions. Here's a detailed guide on how to reach us, whether you're arriving by bus/van or train:

I. From Bus/Van Stations â€º

Most long-distance buses and minivans will drop you off at the main Sihanoukville Bus Terminal or at their specific company offices, usually located near the town center or beaches.

From the Main Sihanoukville Bus Terminal / Central Drop-off Points:

  • Distance: Onederz Sihanoukville is typically within 3-5 kilometers (1.8 - 3.1 miles) from the main bus terminal or most central minivan drop-off points.
  • Travel Time:
    • Tuk-Tuk: 10-20 minutes.
    • Taxi: 5-15 minutes.
  • How to Get Here:
    • Tuk-Tuk: You will find many tuk-tuks waiting at the bus terminal and minivan drop-off areas. Agree on a price before starting your journey. It should be around $3 - $6 USD for a ride to Ochheuteal Beach.
    • PassApp/Grab (Ride-hailing Apps): These apps are available in Sihanoukville and often offer transparent pricing. This can be a convenient option.
II. From Sihanoukville Train Station â€º

The Sihanoukville Train Station is located a bit outside the town center and beach areas.

From Sihanoukville Train Station:

  • Distance: Onederz Sihanoukville is approximately 2-3 kilometers (1.2 - 1.8 miles) from the Sihanoukville Train Station.
  • Travel Time:
    • Tuk-Tuk: 5-10 minutes, depending on traffic.
  • How to Get Here:
    • Tuk-Tuk: This is the most common and convenient way from the train station. You'll find tuk-tuks waiting. Always negotiate the price before you get in. Expect to pay around $2 - $4 USD.
    • PassApp/Grab (Ride-hailing Apps): Using a ride-hailing app is highly recommended for clear pricing from the train station.

General Tips for your arrival:

  • Currency: The local currency is the Cambodian Riel (KHR), but US Dollars (USD) are widely accepted everywhere. ATMs are widely available in Sihanoukville.
  • Onederz Contact: If you encounter any issues or need directions upon arrival, please don't hesitate to call us directly.

Explore Sihanoukville

Discover What Sihanoukville Offers!

Sihanoukville is a vibrant coastal city with plenty to see and do! From beautiful islands to lively nightlife, here's a guide to help you explore during your stay.

Island Adventures

Easily access stunning islands like Koh Rong and Koh Rong Sanloem from the nearby pier. Enjoy white-sand beaches, snorkeling, diving, and breathtaking sunsets.

  • Koh Rong: Famous for its lively party scene, pristine beaches, and various water activities.
  • Koh Rong Sanloem: A more tranquil island, perfect for relaxation, snorkeling, and quiet beaches.

Beach Life & Relaxation

Sihanoukville boasts several beautiful beaches, each with its own unique vibe. Relax on the sand, swim in the clear waters, or enjoy fresh seafood by the shore.

  • Otres Beach: Known for its relaxed atmosphere, beachfront bars, and water sports.
  • Serendipity Beach: A popular hub for dining, nightlife, and boat departures.
  • Independence Beach: A quieter option, great for a peaceful stroll or swim.

City Sights & Nightlife

Explore the city's local markets, enjoy diverse dining options, and experience Sihanoukville's growing nightlife scene.

  • Local Markets: Discover fresh produce, street food, and souvenirs.
  • Restaurants & Bars: A wide range of dining experiences from local Khmer cuisine to international dishes, plus vibrant bars.
  • Golden Lions Roundabout: A central landmark and common meeting point.

Getting Around

Tuk-tuks and ride-hailing apps like PassApp are readily available for convenient transportation to beaches, the pier, and city attractions. Our reception team can help with directions and local tips!

Local Tip: For information on ferry schedules, transportation, or specific recommendations, please ask our friendly reception team. They are happy to provide local insights to help you make the most of your Sihanoukville adventure!

Dorms: Connect & Unwind

We believe a great stay balances privacy with community! Our dorm rooms offer both, with bunk beds designed like cozy boxes that give you your own private space. Inside, you'll find everything you need: an electric socket, a reading lamp, and an accessory case. Even though we're a budget-friendly accommodation, we don't compromise on comfort; our mattresses, blankets, and pillows are as good as those in a hotel. Plus, all our rooms come with well-maintained air-conditioners and exhaust fans to keep you comfortable.

To ensure your peaceful sleep isn't disturbed, we've placed our shared bathrooms conveniently outside the dorm rooms. This means you can get all the rest you need to wake up refreshed and ready to connect with fellow travelers.

Keep your belongings safe and sound in our large lockers, measuring 40cm x 50cm x 80cm. We even provide padlocks if you don't have your own. So, you'll get two keys when you check in: one for your room door and another for your personal locker. We look forward to welcoming you and helping you make the most of your trip!



Private Room: Your Social Base

Discover the perfect balance of personal space and social opportunity in our private rooms. Each room is thoughtfully designed to be your comfortable base, featuring a refreshing air conditioner and peace of mind with your own electronic digital security box. We've included an electric kettle to kickstart your mornings, and bottles of water to keep you hydrated. Stay connected with loved ones or plan your next outing using our free Wifi, and enjoy the luxury of fresh towels and more.

Forget shared facilities! You'll love the convenience of your private bathroom right inside the room. With a clever partition wall and curtain separating the shower booth and toilet, you'll have all the space and privacy needed to get ready for a bustling day of sightseeing or to relax after a fun social evening.
