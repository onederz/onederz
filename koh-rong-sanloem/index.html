WELCOME TO
ONEDERZ KOH RONG SANLOEM

Discover paradise at Onederz Koh Rong Sanloem, nestled on the stunning Saracen Bay. Our highly-rated hostel offers a serene and relaxed atmosphere, perfect for unwinding amidst tropical beauty. Enjoy comfortable accommodations in private rooms or dorms, a beautiful swimming pool, and direct access to the pristine white sand beach. We are consistently praised for our tranquil environment, comfortable facilities, and friendly staff. It's the ideal place to relax, swim, and explore the island's natural wonders like Lazy Beach and the bioluminescent plankton.

Sleep & Relax

Choose from our cozy mixed or female-only dorms featuring privacy curtains, individual power sockets, and reading lights, all with air-conditioning and large secure lockers. Or upgrade to a spacious private room with en-suite bathrooms, air-conditioning, TV, and complimentary toiletries. All options include free high-speed Wi-Fi.

Our Location

Onederz Koh Rong Sanloem is conveniently located on Saracen Bay, just a short walk from the main pier. Our address: Saracen Bay, Koh Rong Sanloem, Cambodia.

Getting Here:

From Sihanoukville: 45-minute to 1.5-hour ferry ride ($15-$25). We're just a short walk from the Saracen Bay pier.
